About Me

Hi my name is Jessie Lin, I am a recent graduate from Hunter College majoring in computer science. A little bit about myself, I am passionate about problem solving and accomplishing goals. I am a fond believer that anything is possible as long as you have an urge to learn.

More About Me

I became interested in computer science after my first computer science class in sophomore year of college and have since been wanting to make an impact in tech. Hence why I became a teaching assistant for the class of 700+ students wanting to inspire more girls to the field because there is a known gender disparity in the industry which can be seen even from my classes. Over the years, I have become interested in different areas of tech which included machine learning, AI, open-source, assistive technology research, robots and design. I cannot wait to see what technology can do for us in the future.

Some of my personal hobbies include reading books, creating projects, dancing and more.

I'm fluent in English and Mandarin.
