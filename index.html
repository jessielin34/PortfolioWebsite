Galaxy Galore

This galaxy-themed website was created by a team of 3 during the 2021 Stony Brook University's Hackathon. It showcases a 3D/2D model of the Solar System utilizing Google APIs, Javascript, and HTML. Our winning site won best UI/UX Design and Best Space App Powered by U.S. Space Force. I collaborated on creating the foundation of the website and mini arcade games such as Space Invaders, Pong, Dino, etc. by using CSS, HTML, and Javascript. This hackathon consisted of over 250 participants.
